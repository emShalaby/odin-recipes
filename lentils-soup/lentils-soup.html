LENTILS SOUP

This warm delicious soup will make your cold winter easier!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 cups of lentils (red or yellow)
  • 5 large spoons of cooking oil
  • 1 large roasted onion
  • 1 peeled carrot chopped to small pieces
  • celery chopped to small pieces
  • 2 cloves of a garlic
  • 8 tablespoons of chicken broth
  • 1 small spoon of ground cumin
  • salt and pepper as needed
  • 1 small onion chopped
  • 1 big spoon of lemon juice

METHOD:

  • We wash the lentils with water, we heat 3 spoons of oil in a pot and we add the roasted onion to it.
  • We add the carrots , celery and garlic , then we mix continuously for about 15 minutes.
  • After adding the lentils and the chicken broth we leave the pot to boil , we leave the pot for about an hour until the lentils soften
  • Add salt , pepper , cumin and do mixing then we leave the pot on the heat for another 10 minutes until its properly mixed.
  • We heat the remaining of the oil in fried pan and we heat the onion until it's brown then remove the onion and throw away the oil
    • Put the lentils soup in a plate , add lemon juice to it and finally decorate it with some lovely brown onions Enjoy the taste and say goodbye to feeling cold in the harsh winter nights
